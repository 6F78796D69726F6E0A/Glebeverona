01 · Welcome

A distinctive Sydney stay

Historic character.
Modern ease.

Built in 1888, Verona House is a heritage-listed Victorian residence on Glebe Point Road.

High ceilings, arched windows, ironwork and period detailing give the house its unmistakable character. Each room is individually styled and includes a queen bed, private ensuite, air conditioning and plush bedding.
