The story

Victorian Glebe

Preserved character, thoughtfully renewed.

Verona House forms part of a group of grand residences connected to the early subdivision of the Toxteth Estate.

The property is attributed to architect and builder David Elphinstone and reflects the neo-classical Italianate style of late nineteenth-century Sydney.

Its double-fronted rendered façade, arched timber windows, wrought iron detailing, tessellated tiles, decorative brackets and classical loggia create a distinctive presence on Glebe Point Road.
